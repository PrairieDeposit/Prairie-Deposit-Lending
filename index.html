Requirements

What You'll Need to Qualify

Our criteria are clear and straightforward — no surprises, no fine print.

Credit Score of 680+

A minimum credit score of 680 demonstrates responsible financial management and helps secure your best loan terms.

Minimum $7,000 Available

You'll need at least $7,000 on hand for your partial down payment contribution and home closing costs.

Canadian Citizen or PR

Applicants must be a Canadian citizen or permanent resident to qualify for our down payment loan program.

Household Income $81,000+

A minimum combined household income of $81,000 per year ensures you'll be comfortable managing your home costs and repayment.

Positive Net Worth

Applicants must demonstrate a positive net worth, showing that your total assets exceed your total liabilities.
